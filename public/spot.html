FAQ

Email and SMS don't work.
It's being fixed. We just had a server move and some things stopped working. Am I being recorded?
No. The video stream never leaves your computer.

What can I use this for?
Get an alert when your pot of water begins to boil. Monitor the hallway leading to your room. The possibilities are endless.

How do I use this?
The instructions are to the right of the application. Essentially, the area highlighted in green is monitored for movement, and alerts are sent out.

Do I need an attached webcam to use this?
Yes.
What are the pictures at the bottom?
When enough motion occurs in the green box to bring the motion value over the threshold specified by the red line, pictures are captured and presented below the main view.

I just see a blank screen. What's going on?
If you just see a blank black screen, right click it, go to settings, click the camera tab, and select a different camera. You probably want USB Class Video. Also, Google Chrome seems to be incompatible with our application.
